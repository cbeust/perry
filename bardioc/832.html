Feb 12, 2003
832 - Station der MV's
The Station of the Metamorphs
H.G. Ewers

Pucky and the other Terrans arrive on Goshmo's Castle and learn It's plan: the Concepts are going to split the planet in two and one half will become the new home of the seven billion Concepts. Atlan makes contact with some of the Metamorphs on Goshmo's Castle and he learns about the Law, the principles that the Gys Voolberath live for. Inside the station, the Metamorphs are attacked by the positronic guardian of the station and realize that they have to leave the Ganuhr galaxy or they will be destroyed.

Atlan decides to leave the Concepts on Goshmo's Castle and to look for Perry Rhodan, whose crystal has been pulsing recently.

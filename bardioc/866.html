May 6, 2003
866 - Aura des Unheils
Aura of Misery
H.G. Ewers

Realizing his mistake, Hotrenor-Taak frees Tengri Lethos and the two men realize that some Metamorphs are already on board the Base. Passing as members of a delegation from the Gavok, they gain access to the Base and start looking for them. The two men finally locate the Metamorphs as they are in the same room as Demeter. The young woman seems to be emitting an Tba aura that destabilizes the fake appearance of the Metamorphs. They manage to escape before being captured.

Soon thereafter, Tengri Lethos receives from It the mission to look for his Eternity Ship.

Mar 24, 2003
845 - Treibgut der Sterne
Flotsam of the Stars
Hans Kneifel

A spaceship of mercenaries who live off collecting space debris arrives in the vicinity of Arcur Beta, hoping to recover technology left behind by the Larens. They rescue Hotrenor-Taak, who pretends to be a simple soldier. The Laren commander deceives them and manages to take control of their spaceship. He then flies toward Olymp.

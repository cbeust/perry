320 - Operation Blitz
Operation Blitz
Clark Darlton

The Operation Blitz starts: the goal is to locate and destroy the main crystal on Danger I. After several obstacles, the Terrans manage to find it deep under the surface and thanks to Pucky's help, the crystal is destroyed. When the destruction occurs, a hyper-impulse is sent out causing both the main crystal and the twenty-seven crystals on the other planets to turn into howalgonium.

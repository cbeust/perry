338 - Die stählerne Zitadelle
The Steel Fortress
338

The Khrest arrives on Truuktan, where the SOS came from. The Terrans discover a worlds mostly agricultural, except for a huge fortress, where the Terrans assume the Skovarto is being kept prisoner. To Rhodan's surprise, Tolot and Teik tell him they don't want to be part of this mission. Instead, the Halutians leave on some mysterious exploration mission.

Rhodan and Marshall, disguised as Kobalts -- the name they gave to the native, who sport a blue skin -- try to enter the fortress but they are captured. Tschubai tries to help them but his attempts fail: the fortress is protected against teleporters.

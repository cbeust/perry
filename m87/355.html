Jul 22, 2002
355 - Der Doppelagent von Rumal
The Double Agent from Rumal
William Voltz

The Rumalian USO specialist Vito Batabano has been spying on the Akonids for several years. He informs the Terrans of his latest discovery: the Akonids made a unique copy of the Vega time transmitter and intend to fit it on a ship and then send this ship in the Terran empire to proceed to a time experiment. The Akonids hope to convince the Time Police that the Terrans are the real perpetrators of the time travel infractions.

The Terrans turn the plan around and capture the spaceship. Then they take it into Akonid territory and start the transmitter there. The device is destroyed in the process.

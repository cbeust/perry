Nov 6, 2002
365 - Das strahlende Netz
The Radiating Net
Hans Kneifel

The Skovarto directs the Krest IV toward Zootkohn, the first planet of Dusty Queen. The planet is surrounded by an energetic net that forces all Beasts away from it, including Icho Tolot and Fancan Teik, who stay away on their spaceship. Unharmed by the radiation, the Terrans land on Zootkohn.

On the planet, they discover Beasts wearing protection suits to insulate them from the radiations. Several thousands Skovars are enslaved and their eggs are being genetically manipulated by the Beasts in order to produce new Beasts and also Dolans. The Beasts are also using hypnotive suggestions on sixty-two Okefenokees so that they operate the facilities.

The Terrans attack Zootkohn, destroy the plant and the Dolans and free the Okfenokees. A failsafe mechanism causes the destruction of the planet.

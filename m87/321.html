Nov 11, 2000
321 - Zwischenfall im Tiger-Sektor
Trap in the Tiger Sector
H.G. Ewers

The second phase of the Operation Blitz is to destroy the central commands that program the crystals. The eight planets hosting these installations are now known by the Terrans and all targets are attacked simultaneously.

The main offensive takes place on Modula II, where all the Perlians are quickly found and killed. However, five Perlian spaceships manage to escape. They are protected by ruby fields that are insensible to Terran weapons. The protection fields disrupt the Terran linear drives, causing the pursuit to end abruptly. Before the Perlian spaceships escape, five Terrans have the time to teleport on board and manage to destroy them all.

Jul 20, 2002
348 - Die Sucher von M-87
The Researcher of M-87
H.G. Ewers

Forty-eight hours after Perry Rhodan's ultimatum, the Terrans meet with Agen Thrumb's emissary, Kibos Baiwof, in the Dewell system. The Terrans show a staged film depicting the planet of the Bestians. Rhodan demands a transdim engine in exchange for the coordinates of the Bestian homeworld. Suddenly, a bomb hidden in the base engineer's spacesuit explodes, paralyzing the entire crew of the Crest IV, which is made prisoner on Thrumb's space station. However, Tschubai and Pucky manage to teleport in safety.

The mutants manage to damage the space station's engine, and the linear flight is interrupted.

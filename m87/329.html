May 29, 2001
329 - Ein Planet läuft Amok
A Planet run Amok
H.G. Ewers

On January 25th, 2436, the Khrest IV runs out of water. The Terrans finally discover a planet, called Clearwater, that seems to hold enough water for everyone. The Khrest IV empties its tanks into the sea and fills up. Shortly after, they are under attack by all kinds of creatures on the planet.

They understand that the planet and its fauna live in symbiosis and that the fact that they dumped the poisoned water into the sea was deemed an act of hostility. The Terrans reach a truce with the planet and are asked to leave immediately. They fill up on water and leave.

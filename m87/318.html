November 11, 2000
318 - Die Zentrale der Freischärler
The Partisans' Central Command
Conrad Shepherd

The spaceship holding the Terrans prisoners reach Menschos, the central planet of the Gurrads. The Gurrads are still suspicious about the Terrans and keep questioning them. Rhodan, Melbar Kason and Oro Masut manage to escape and try to help their fellow prisoners. The Terrans meet with the Gurrads'rulers: a trio of three people, a Gurrad, a General and a Shangant, a race derived from the Gurrads.

A hypercom message is sent to an unknown recipient, outside the Menschos system. The Gurrads immediately suspect the Terrans and the ensuing misunderstanding end up in a fight causing several deaths.

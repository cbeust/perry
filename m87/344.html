Jan 6, 2002
344 - Die Kidnapper des Auserwählten
The Kidnapper of the Chosen One
Clark Darlton

On the secluded continent, Rhodan locates a Base Engineer called Agen Thrum. The creature spends most of its time protected by a spherical energy shield, but at night, as the Terrans launch their offensive, they catch a glimpse of the appearance of the engineer: he looks like a Halutian except for the colors of his skin and also the fact that he has twenty-two stones engraved in his chest.

The Terrans manage to paralyze the engineer and, using a diversion caused by the Crest who fakes an attack on Geegival, the Terrans bring the Base Engineer in captivity on the Krest.

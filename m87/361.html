Aug 1, 2002
361 - Der Turm des ewigen Lebens
The Tower of Eternal Life
Clark Darlton

The Okefenokees mourn their deads in a funeral in which the dead bodies are put in coffins that take off and disappear toward the center of the galaxy. Atlan dispatches the KC-31 to follow the coffins in order to find out where they are headed. After a short journey involving a sudden acceleration due to the Absolute Movement, the KC-31 reaches a planet made entirely of neo-howalgonium which they call Monol. The vast quantities of crystal seem to convert the radiance from the center of M-87 into powerful five dimensional radiations.

Pucky teleports on the surface and eventually reaches a tower where he is absorbed by a phenomenon that totally rejuvenates him.

Nov 17, 2002
366 - Das Rätsel der Biostation
The Riddle of the Biostation
Clark Darlton

On microfilms found on Zootkohn, Rhodan learns of the Bourjaily system, another central planet of the Bestiands. Eynch Zigulor, the leader of the Okefenokees freed on Zootkohn, asks Rhodan to destroy this planet, but Rhodan refuses and wants to investigate it first.

The Krest reaches Ednil, one of the planets of Bouraily. The planet is an aquatic world and the Terrans discover a submarine world, where they defeat several hundred Bestians. They discover Perlians who explain to the Terrans that their race has been enslaved by the Bestians for centuries. They get fitted with a temporal eye collected from a monster jellyfish-like creature that lives on Ednil, and then sent to an unknown destination. Rhodan explains to them that this unknown destination is the Magellanic Clouds where their descendants are allied with the Bestians.

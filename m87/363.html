Nov 6, 2002
363 - Nacht zwischen den Sonnen
The Night between the Suns
H.G. Ewers

Olukh gives Rhodan the coordinates of a dark cloud called Lethara, which is the central system of the Beasts. Olukh dies shortly thereafter, victim of a metabolism accelerator that was put on his body by the Okefenokees during the battle.

The Krest enters the dark cloud and finds out that it is populated by proto-algae. They find a red sun which they name Dusty Queen around which orbit three planets: Zootkohn, Pauper and Norvo. A commando lands on Norvo, which is populated by gigantic creatures with skins that are similar to Dolans'.

839 - Das groﬂe Feuerwerk
The Big Firework
Kurt Mahr

The three billion Concepts materialize on the Earth instead of Eden II. It turns out that before the Earth entered the Pharynx, It had given precise instructions to Nathan to stop its normal functions as long as at least two billion human beings are detected on the Earth. By teleporting the Concepts on the Earth, It forces Nathan to resume its functions. Soon, the climate control is restored and the computer resumes producing spaceships.

Trevor Casalle, the former Aphilian dictator, manages to overcome his dual personality and attempts to restore the reign of the Pure Reason. Chased by thousands of Terrans, he is eventually captured and killed.

The concepts disappear and rematerialize on Eden II. Nathan then starts preparing the Earth for a future repopulation.

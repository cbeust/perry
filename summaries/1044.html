1044 - Die schwarze Macht
The Black Power
H.G. Francis

With the help of the glove, Icho Tolot and Bruke Athosen take control of the Base and they corrupt the Hamiller Tube so it can no longer fight them. Their plan is eventually foiled and Tolot and Athosen flee on the Halutian spaceship.

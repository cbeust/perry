June 17th, 2004
1146 - Angriff der Barbaren
Attack of the Barbarians
Detlev G. Winter

Jercygehl An tells Rhodan that these spaceships belong to the Torkrotians, also called Barbarians. Their goal is to randomly attack spaceships in the Armada in order to make sure they are still efficient. The recent silence from the Armada Heart seems to have plunged them into chaos and they are now out of control.

Atlan presents himself as a messenger from the Armada Heart. Initially skeptical, the Barbarians recognize him as such and dispatch Reihum, one of their commanders, on the Sol in order to assist the Terrans in their investigation on the whereabouts of the Armada Heart.

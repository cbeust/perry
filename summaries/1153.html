July 16th, 2004
1153 - Hölle auf Erden
Hell on Earth
H.G. Ewers

The Earth and the Moon are drifting in the Grey Corridor, surrounded by color-changing walls. A Tsunami is sent to try and break through the wall but the attempt fails. The Earth is sent in the past during the early days of the Third Power and Chthon announces that the second plague is on its way toward the Earth.

The plague manifests itself in the form of parasites that start infesting humans throughout the Earth. The infected people call themselves Dordonians and no longer consider themselves Terrans. The parasites start connecting to each other and form organisms growing bigger every day.

In a research lab, a scientist discovers that the smallpox virus is capable of destroying the parasites and the second plague is soon obliterated.

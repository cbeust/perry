August 12th, 2004
1171 - Der Todesimpuls
The Death Impulse
Clark Darlton

When Seth Apophis died, she released her more powerful psychic impulse in order to harm It. On Eden II, twelve gnomes appear out of nowhere and take possession of the consciousnesses of several mutants, including Tako Kakuta. It gives a body to Ralph Marten and, using the help from Harno and from an individual called Pertrex, manages to fight off the invaders.

As the gnomes are killed, their consciousness penetrates inside It and start attacking him from the inside, but Pertrex manages to destroy them using a psychic net materialized by It.

Nov 11, 2003
1006 - Die Falle von Cratcan
The Cratcan Trap
Clark Darlton

Mallagan, Faddon and Scoutie arrive on Felloy where they receive more Scouties and are then sent on a mission by the Aychartanians, who suspect them to be spies. They are teamed with creatures called Tarts and go on a research station that has recently stopped transmitting. The station has been abandoned and seems to have been destroyed by Kanimoore torpedoes, a race hostile to the Aychartanians.

The Betschidians return to the main base and are then sent to the Nest of the Seventeenth Fleet.

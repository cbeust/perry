May 6, 2003
864 - Demeters Flucht
Demeter's Escape
H.G. Francis

Hamiller meets with three mutants chasing Boyt Margor and is relieved to realize that they know he is under his influence. Kershyll Vanne arrives at the dig in Crete and barely escapes from an explosion caused by someone working for Margor.

Demeter manages to escape from Boyt Margor and starts exploring various cities on the Earth. She is eventually contacted by the three mutants who tell her look out for Margor. The young woman takes the name Dunja Varenczy.

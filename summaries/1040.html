Dec 10, 2003
1040 - Unheil über Kran
Disaster on Kran
Kurt Mahr

Carnuum launches an attack against the Oracle. He wants to take control of Kran and then destroy the Brotherhood. Violent fights erupt on Kran.

On the Sol, Scoutie and Faddon discover the mutilated of Douc Langur, who was attacked when he tried to deactivate Seneca. Mallagan is contacted by the Oracle who tells him that he has been manipulated by the Brotherhood. Mallagan awakes from his hypnosis and is back to his former self. The Sol fires on the palace of the Oracle in order to frighten the attackers.

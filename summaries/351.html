Jul 24, 2002
351 - Der versklavte Riese
The Enslaved Giant
William Voltz

At the end of the battle, the Terrans spot a Dolan that seems to follow an erratic trajectory. The Dolan activates its transdim engine, closely followed by the Terrans. The pursuit ends in a distant arm of the Milky Way, and Camaron Olek, who's on board the pursuing ship, realizes that it is Tro Khon himself who seems to be in difficulty.

The Terrans teleport on the Dolan but Tro Khon, who is controlled by his symbiotic partner, wants to make them his executors. The Terrans manage to paralyze the symbiot and Tro Khon lets the Terrans go during the short time of lucidity he gets.

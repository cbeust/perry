1022 - Der Held von Arxisto
The Hero from Arxisto
Ernst Vlcek

On the planet Arxisto, in M-13, heaps of matter materialize out of nowhere and creatures shaped from that matter start attacking the residents, using their acid skin and tentacles. Different creatures appear, looking like dragonflies. They have weapons and they cause a lot of destruction on the planet.

The Tsunami-97 and Tsunami-36, on which Tolot is hidden, arrive on Arxisto and they observe a strange phenomenon: two gigantic golden-looking rails in the form of a Y are hovering in mid-space.

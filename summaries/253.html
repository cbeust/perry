253 VorstoB in die Dunkelwelt H. G. Ewers With the Terran crew under their complete hypnotic control, the Bio-Decomposers force the Khrest III to go to Ollura. Rhodan follows them there and with Kalak's help, he can gain control of the Khrest III and free his men. But tough fights ensue and all the Bio-Decomposers must be exterminated. Rhodan fears that the Masters of the Island might be alerted that the Terrans have entered their territory.

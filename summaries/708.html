708 - Zwischenspiel auf Saturn
Interlude on Saturn
Clark Darlton

Tekener and two other prisoners escape from Titan and flee on board of a stolen spaceship in the rings of the planet. They are joined by the three Mucies but before they can finally leave the Sol system, the Heavies locate them and capture them all. The six men are brought back on Titan.

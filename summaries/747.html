747 - Die Körperlosen von Grosocht
The Bodyless from Grosocht
Harvey Patton

Felmer Lloyd and Pucky arrive on the planet where the emergency signal was sent. The natives are harmless people but they are ruled by a very strong psychic emitter that the two mutants eventually locate.

The pseudo-divinity is a psychic being who is using the body of an Ilt to rule the planet. Pucky frees his compatriot who dies shortly after. The entity is destroyed and the planet is freed from its tyran.

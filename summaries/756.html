756 - Ein Stern funkt SOS
Interstellar SOS
William Voltz

On Ovaron's planet, Bully decides to try to modify a nearby star so that it emits an SOS with five-dimensional waves. In order to convince the government of the planet to help him, Bully offers to give them the Lemurian spaceships that arrived through the Pharynx a few months ago.

When they reach the location where the Lemurian fleet is, they find out that most of the fleet is unusable and they can only bring back a few ships. Meanwhile, a device is installed in the sun which starts sending its interstellar SOS.

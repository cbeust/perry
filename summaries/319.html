Nov 11, 2000
319 - Der Phantomsender
The Phantom Transmitter
Conrad Shepherd

Informed about the situation in the Gurrad camp thanks to a mini-emitter hidden on Roi Danton, Rhodan decides to try and find the hypercom radio. The mysterious emitter seems to move after each transmission, leading Rhodan to believe that they are using a teleporter.

Rhodan, Masut and Kasom eventually catch up with the transmitter and kill the two generals manipulating it. Rhodan goes to the Gurrad central command and turns the transmitter in, in an attempt to prove the Terrans' will to cooperate with them.

As a thank you gift, the Gurrads give the Terrans the coordinates to twenty-eight planets that host main crystals.

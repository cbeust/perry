March 29th, 2003
1097 - Begegnung in der Unendlichkeit
Encounter in Infinity
H.G. Francis

Icho Tolot and the rebels enter the cosmic platform where they meet Auerspor, which the Halutian manages to lure into a trap. The fight turns to Tolot's advantage the and Dismantler is killed.

At the same time, Beverly Freden and Andrej Sokonic, still looking for the Stac, think they have finally found their destination as they reach the inside of the Spinning Void. Tolot locates them and Freden and Sokonic take him on board.

1027 - Das Superspiel
The Super Game
William Voltz

The game takes place in a cubic building made of sixty-four rooms, all of them isolated and certain of them connected with a transmitter. Mallagan realizes that the Lugosiade is a way for the organizers to detect candidates with superior abilities. The walls become transparent and he becomes aware of the layout of the rooms, as is Doevelynk, another candidate. The game is now turning into a duel between Mallagan and Doevelynk.

Mallagan eventually triumphs and he gets dematerialized. Faddon and Scoutie, who are anxiously waiting to hear from their friend, realize that Mallagan is no longer in the building.

762 - Aufstand der Cyborgs
Revolt of the Cyborgs
H.G. Ewers

When they realize that Rhodan won't acknowledge their newly-gained humanity, the Mucy's turn to the Larens. They send a delegation to Hotrenor-Taak and demand sovereignty for Yolschor, the dark cloud that hosts the planetary system where all Mucy's live.

Jan 24, 2003
1060 - Der Planet Vulkan
The Planet Vulcan
Clark Darlton

Pucky and Ras Tschubai show signs of exhaustion since they have been back from Em. A crewmember called Mirko Hannemas tells him that he just had the vision of a volcano on a nearby planet. Without telling anyone, Pucky locates the planet and teleports there. He discovers the Maringos, a race of humanoids torn apart by tribal wars. Pucky restores the peace and is soon attracted by impulses coming from the main volcano that remind him those he perceived on Em.

As he gets close to the center of the volcano, Pucky falls asleep. Ras Tschubai locates him and brings him back on the Dan Picot.

March 5, 2003
1078 - Die Seth-Apophis-Brigade
The Seth Apophis Brigade
Kurt Mahr

On their way back to the Solar System, the Terran fleet receives a radio signal from the Sol and Rhodan learns about Atlan's return. The two men meet on a planet but even Pucky can't see that Atlan and his crew are now under Seth Apophis' influence.

The Rakal Woolver makes a stop on an inhabited planet where most of the Terrans are dropped off. Then it resumes its course toward the Solar System where it receives the authorization to land on the Earth.

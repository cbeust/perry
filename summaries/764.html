Jan 6, 2002
764 - Der Wall um die Welt
The Barrier around the World
Clark Darlton

Icho Tolot and Lraton Perlat arrive in the Nephrem system. Tolot tells his companion that he has reasons to believe that the second planet of this system hosts one of the four cell activators that It once scattered in the galaxy but that were never found.

On the planet, they are attacked by a group of pirates who crashed several years ago. The chief of the rebels carries the cell-activator and the Halutians are forced to fight him. Helped with the local resistance, they overpower the pirates and Tolot receives the cell activator.

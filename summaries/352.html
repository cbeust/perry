Jul 22, 2002
352 - Der Planet des tödlichen Schweigens
The Planet of the Deadly Silence
Hans Kneifel

The Ex-2333, an explorer ship, approaches the second planet of the sun Kyrou. The Terrans discover the remnants of the cruiser Silver Dart and also the ruins of the colonial city Magellan, which has been totally destroyed. The two million settlers are nowhere to be found.

They discover survivors who have found shelter under the surface. The city has been destroyed by a Dolan. Soon thereafter, more Dolans attack the planet and destroy the Ex-2333. The Terrans get rescued a few days later by a rescue team. The attack on the Kyro system is not an isolated system: the Dolans are now launching a full attack against all Terran planets.

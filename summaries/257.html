257 Der Dreitöter William Voltz Rhodan decides to go on Tefrod, the Tefrodian's main planet, to gather information on the Masters of the Island. But the planet is closely protected and the mutants do not represent a significant advantage over the Tefrodians since these also have mutants. The expedition is a complete failure, but Rhodan and the commando are able to escape. Rhodan learns that no Tefrodian has ever seen a Master of the Island, except for their chief, the Virth, who lives in an extremely well protected island.

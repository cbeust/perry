July 16th, 2004
1149 - Im Bann des Zweisterns
Under the influence of the double star
Marianne Sydow

Following the direction of the Womme, the Terran fleet arrives on the planet Carmen where they make contact where the natives, who live in symbiosis with the flora. They move around riding flying flowers and they follow directions coming from the double sun. However, these directions have recently stopped, which makes Rhodan realize that the creatures are under the influence of Seth Apophis.

The Terrans restore the impulses from the double star and return to the Base.

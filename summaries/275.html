275 Der Flug nach Barkon Clark Darlton The Masters of the Island, alerted that the Vega transmitter was used, take advantage of the arrival on the Earth of the Terran commando to lay out a trap for the Khrest III. One of them, Toser-Ban, uses the commando as a bait and attracts the Khrest III on the Earth, where he tries to destroy it. Thanks to a lucky turn of events, the trap fails and the Terran commando is safely rescued.

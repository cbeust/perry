May 22, 2001
735 - Dir Armee aus dem Ghetto
The army from the Ghetto
Kurt Mahr

Casalle tortures Sergio Percellar and manages to learn the location of Porta Pato, the secret base of the rebels. The base is conquered by the Aphilians but Bully and all the members from Ovaron escape. They are hidden by Father Ironside, who built his own parallel network of resistants. With the help of the inhabitants of Goshmo Castle, they manage to free Sergio Percellar.

May 22, 2001
734 - Operation Gehirnwäsche
Operation Brainwash
Kurt Mahr

The Aphilians are trying to build a gigant hypnotizing field designed to wipe the memory of all Terrans, so that they forget all about their previous non-aphilian condition. Thanks to its spies, the OGN learns about the operation and sends a commando to destroy the site. The operation fails and Sergio Percellar is captured, although the resistance thinks he died in the action.

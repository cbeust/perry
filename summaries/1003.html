Nov 11, 2003
1003 - Neulinge an Bord
Rookies on Board
Petter Terrid

The Arsalom is attacked by pirates called Aychartanians. The Beschidians are instrumental in the confrontation to fight them back. However, the Krane commander, Mallagan, suspects that a saboteur is on board.

The Arsalom reaches its goal, the Nest of the Eighth Fleet, where the Beschidians undergo additional military training and get the attention of Czyk, the commander of the Tenth Fleet. The saboteur is soon discovered and a pseudo-Spoodie is discovered on him, from which he apparently received instructions.

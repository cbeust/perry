Mar 30, 2000
707 - Der Arenakämpfer
The Arena Fighter
H.G. Ewers

The three Mucys brought by the Doogen, who host the consciousness of Wuriu Sengu, Tako Kakuta and Betty Toufry, land on Mars on board the Kehatron. There, they see Ronald Tekener being arrested because he refused to take part in an arena fight. Until then, Tekener had been able to successfully elude the Heavies under the identity of Kalteen Marquanteurs.

The three cyborgs learn that Tekener has been taken to Titanium, a Saturn moon, and they decide to follow him there.

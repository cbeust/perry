Jan 20, 2003
1057 - Die Gestrandeten
Shipwrecked
H.G. Francis

Icho Tolot and Bruke Athosien realize that their ship won't be able to reach the mysterious double pulsar. On Seth Apophis' order, they head for Lookout, the space station between the Milky Way and Andromeda. They realize that the station has been invaded by a race of protoplasmic creatures. Tolot escapes temporarily from Seth Apophis' influence and he helps the Terrans and Maahks to chase the invaders from the station.

Tolot and Athosien then leave and resume their course, now informed that their destination is not the place called the Warehouse but a simple reflector designed to confuse Seth Apophis' enemies. The glove worn by Tolot creates a singularity in space and they are hurled in an unkown region of space where they stand in front of a gigantic crystal wall.

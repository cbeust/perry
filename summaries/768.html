May 23, 2002
768 - Terra-Patrouille
Terra Patrol
William Voltz

On March 4th, 3582, Alaska Saedelaere, Douc Langur and a group of people create the Terra Patrol, whose goal is to determine where the Earth is and to reactivate Nathan in attempt to regain control of the weather.

The next day, Langur flies to the Moon in order to try to fix Nathan, and he realizes on the way that he can move through space without wearing a spacesuit, making him wonder even more if he is a human or a robot. On the Moon, Langur manages to penetrate in Nathan but fails to reactivate the giant computer.

Meanwhile the manifesto of the Terra patrol is emitted in space and it is received by the messengers of the Incarnation Clermac, who decide to examine the planet surface.

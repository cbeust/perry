849 - Sprung über den Abgrund
Jump over the Abyss
Kurt Mahr

The second half of Eden II turns into pure energy and the Concepts begin their journey. As the transfer of the Earth draws near, most of the humans move to the Moon, which will be protected by its paratron field during the transition, but their fears are baseless: on June 28th, the Earth and the Moon rematerialize in the Solar System.

212 Die Mikro-Festung William Voltz In order to restore the ship's initial size, Rhodan sends a commando to the south pole to destroy the dome. The commando is using antique jets, which engines don't seem to be affected by the reduction field. However, the aircrafts are attacked by flying robots and their pilots only barely make it back to the Khrest II. In the meantime, Rhodan learns that the domes were created during the war that once opposed the inhabitants of the yellow floor and those on the surface. The two people exterminated each other and only the robots remained on the surface.

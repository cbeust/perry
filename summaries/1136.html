June 8th, 2004
1136 - Die letzten Maahks
The Last Maahks
William Voltz

Grek-336 is a Maahk from a distant future. Chased by the Shadow Maahks, he hides in a transmitter station and a malfunction sends him back into the past. He arrives on the Earth during the attack of the Parsfs and after misunderstanding the message of an evangelist, starts wondering if the Terrans are not at the origin of the Shadow Maahks, who will later threaten the entire Maahk race by pushing them toward desincarnation.

Grek-336 decides to fight the Terrans and his mental waves are received by Vishna, who finally finds an ally beyond the anti-temporal field.

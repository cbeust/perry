March 30th, 2004
401 - Aufbruch ins All
Departure into Space
William Voltz

The Saparians are descendants from Terrans who settled on Conyers, the moon of a giant planet called Firmer. When their civilization reaches the age of space travel, they try to land on Firmer. They discover agents of the Dabrifa Imperium who plan on experimenting a new weapon, the Emotional Radiant, on them.

In the meantime, the Solar Defense has learned about Dabrifa establishing a secret base on Firmer and sends a fleet there. The Saparians are rescued and Dabrifa's men are arrested.

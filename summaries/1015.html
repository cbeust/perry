Nov 19, 2003
1015 - Das Schiff der Ahnen
The Ship of the Elders
Marianne Sydow

The three Betschidians find evidence of Spoodies on board and as they search through the spaceship, they find hundreds of human skeletons. They activate a screen showing a scene where the Iktor is approaching a galaxy. A humanoid is reporting to the commander of the vessel, a humanoide with a long white hair who is addressed as "Atlan".

A fleet of Krane spaceships arrives on Krane Trap and, protected by their shields, they remain immune to the psychic impulses. They narrow in on the wreck of the Iktor and capture the Betschidians.

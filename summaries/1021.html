Nov 24, 2003
1021 - Der unsichtbare Gegner
The Invisible Opponent
H.G. Francis

An unknown force takes control of Icho Tolot who starts attacking his surrounding during a Halutian art exhibition. Tolot is eventually paralyzed by a commando led by Felmer Lloyd, who has noticed some external influence but without being able to pinpoint it.

At the same momen, Bruke Atosen is activated, becoming an agent from Seth Apophis. He escapes and rescues Tolot, who manages to hide on board of a ship part of the Tsunami fleet.

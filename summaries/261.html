261 Die Fabrik des Teufels Kurt Mahr The Terrans realize that the Tefrodian transmitter actually sent them within the forbidden area. As the Khrest III is hiding near a planetary system, the Terrans discover a strange planet, inhabited by immortal humans that were kidnapped throughout different eras on the Earth. These Terrans, completely apathetic, seem to have lost all notions of time. Some have been living on this planet -- called History -- for centuries. This human zoo is guarded by Tefrodians who are all alike.

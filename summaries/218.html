218 Brennpunkt Twin H. G. Ewers The flying fortress of the Maahks attacks Raum/Quinta. It is protected by an undestructible green field and uses the dreadful Converter Guns. The Terrans fight with Transformer Guns and giga Arkonid bombs, the only weapons that seem to be able to get through the force field. They manage to keep the fortress away and stay in control of the Twin transmitter.

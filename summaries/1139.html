June 8th, 2004
1139 - Unheimliches Erwachen
Dangerous awakening
Ernst Vlcek

In order to understand the behavior of Grek-336, the Terrans decide to investigate the Maahk population. And expedition led by Tekener is sent toward that space stations in the big void. During the flight, mysterious organisms adhere to the hull of the Tsunami spaceships, and further analysis reveals that they were spread by the Parsfs during their offensive against the Earth.

The Terrans reach Maahkora and discover thousands of Maahks on stasis. The organisms wake the Maahks up who then proceed to attack the Terrans. The Terrans manage to destroy the mysterious entities and the Maahks return into stasis.

Jan 6, 2002
343 - Planet der tausend Freuden
The Planet of a thousand Joys
William Voltz

In search for more clues about the rulers of M-87, the Krest heads toward Geegival, a leisure planet that is said to receive frequent visits from important people from M-87. Rhodan and a small group impersonate a crew of Blues and land on the planet.

After complaining repeatedly about the conditions of their stay, they are taken in front of the person in charge of Geegival, an insect-nature called the Fleel Jinguisem. The bee-like creature accepts to transfer the Terrans onto a continent where only very high executives have access. Rhodan hopes that he will be able to meet with a Base Engineer, one of the highly placed personalities in M-87.

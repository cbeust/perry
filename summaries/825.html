825 - Die Amokmacher
The Chaos Maker
H.G. Francis

The Halutians on Terzrock have reverted to their ancestors state, the Beasts, and they show extreme signs of aggressivity. Also on the planet are deported Halutians, who are normal but were transfered on the planet because of their unusual size.

Tekener and Thyron escape and hide in a crystal forest, using the assistance of Bakor-Tars, a normal Halutian. They meet Gurrads in the forest and team up with them.

The Armada Raft
Thomas Ziegler

The Armada Raft Crduun is shaken by violent fights opposing Fain, who was once captured by a collector robot from his planet, to the commander of the Raft. The Raft is soon rendered almost useless and Fain tries to deliver it to Rhodan but dies in the attempt. The only survivor, Crduun, tries to keep the Raft together and sends an SOS.

Rhodan decides to attack the Synchrodrome with a small fleet in order to destroy all the Synchronites.

Nov 11, 2003
1004 - Die Stufen der Erkenntnis
The Steps to Knowledge
Kurt Mahr

Mallagan, Faddon and Scoutie leave on a mission to find Aychartanian bases on the spaceship Santonmar. They land on a planet called St-Vain, after the name of the Betschidian leader and face various tests. They realize that this is part of their training until they get captured by real pirates.

May 3rd, 2004
1113 - Die Station des Silbernen
The Station of the Silvery
H.G. Francis

When the Terrans refuse to cooperate, Shovkrodon has them attacked but with the help of the teleporters, they manage to destroy the robots the Silvery sends. Shovrkodon realizes that he is at a disadvantage because he doesn't have a lot of them, and he doesn't want to damage the space station, which he uses to reprogram Armada Monteurs. He manages to escape and the Terrans return to the Base.

May 3rd, 2004
1118 - Der Admiral und der Silberne
The Admiral and the Silvery
K.H. Scheer

Clifton Callamon is captured by the Armadasmith Noschenhew, who asks him to go on a mission on a planet orbiting a blue sun. A data collector spaceship crashed on that planet and Callamon must rescue the information it collected. The mission fails and Noschenhew condems Callamon and his men to die on their spaceships, the Sodome, which is now drifting helplessly toward the blue sun.

The Base appears, informed of the situation by a White Raven, and Callamon and his men are rescued. The Admiral gives Perry Rhodan the information he secretly collected on the crashed spaceship, as the Sodome disappears into the blue sun.

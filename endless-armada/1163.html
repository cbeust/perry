1163 - Invasion der Fairy Queens
Invasion of the Fairy Queens
Thomas Ziegler

Chthon, who seems to know Vishna, announces the fifth Plague: love. Small creatures that are fifteen centimeter tall appear on the Earth. They are called Fairy Queens and are projections from a different universe. Vishna's goal is to capture the souls of all the Terrans in order to bring them into her own universe.

Each Fairy Queen associates itself with a human and establishes a strong connection casting its victim into a subjugated state. Even Nathan is under attack, but rescued by Bull at the last minute. The Psi Cartel intervenes and manages to break the relationships between the Fairy Queens and the Terrans, who slowly recover their freedom.

August 16th, 2004
1152 - Gespensterwelt
The Ghost World
Ernst Vlcek

The Rakal Woolver arrives the Solar System, one month after leaving M-3. The Terrans find the Earth deserted and displaced compared to its normal orbit. When they land on the Earth, they discover ghostly human shapes. The shapes explain to them that they are actually on the Projected Earth. Soon, Danton and Demeter learn what happened to the real Earth and Moon.

Taurec tries to locate the Earth with the advanced equipment on board his Syzzel but fails. The Projected Earth starts dissolving along with the ghosts that inhabit it.

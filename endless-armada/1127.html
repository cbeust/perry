May 13, 2004
1127 - Die Ewigen Diener
The Eternal Servants
Marianne Sydow

Using the knowledge contained inside the Viral Empire, Belice learns about the Klongs, a race living in a place called Klongheim which is looking for their lost leader, supposed to lead them to the Command Code. Belice sends two androides on their world and learns that the Klongs are at war with another race, the Psarfs.

Belice builds an entity which is then recognized by both the Klongs and the Psarfs as their lost leader. They receive the order to fly toward the Earth and move it off its orbit so it can be placed under Belice's control.

June 17th, 2004
1144 - Operation Hornissenschwarm
Operation Hornet Swarm
H.G. Francis

The Blind Ones have been banned by Meegoron, the Armadasmith in charge of the space station, and decide to help the Terrans. Outside the embankment, Perry Rhodan sends a fleet of five thousand ships led by Clifton Callamon to rescue Pucky, Jen Salik and Ras Tschubai.

Some of the Gazelles manage to penetrate inside the sphere and then face one thousand Pellacks that Meegoron has charged up with psychic energy. With Pucky's help, the Pellacks are defeated and the Terrans return to the Galactic Fleet.

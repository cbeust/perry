1132 - Die Toten und der Wächter
The Dead and the Watcher
H.G. Ewers

Rhodan and Boon form an alliance and together, they climb the steps of the pyramid Arratur. At the top of the pyramid, they listen to a message from the consciousness of one of the creators of the emitters. They learn the location of the command central of the emitter but when they get there, they must fight its guardian, who wants to call Seth Apophis. They manage to destroy him and the whole palace collapses.

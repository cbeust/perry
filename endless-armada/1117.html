May 3rd, 2004
1117 - Das Gedankenmonster
The Thought Monster
H.G. Ewers

Many participants of the psychic block are hit by a shock and start acting violently. Bull finds Ellert as he regains control of Coolafe's mind. He asks Tifflor to call off the destruction of the plasma because it would be irreplaceable. Ellert understands that the side effects of the operation are caused by an incompatibility between the human minds and the consciousnesses inside It. He manages to refocus the projector and perfect copies of the Earth and the Moon suddenly materialize.

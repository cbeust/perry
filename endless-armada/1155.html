July 16th, 2004
1155 - Der Erwecker
The Awakener
Arndt Ellmer

Bright lights announce the arrival of the Third Plague, which Chthon describes as the Shadow of Death.

In Australia, a monk called Le So Te starts getting popular by claiming he has the power of resurrecting the dead. Bull keeps Le So Te under close surveilance, thinking he is a messenger from It. Le So Te's influence becomes more influential as he resurrects Thora, who, strangely, has memories dating from before her time.

Galbraith Deighton is now convinced that Le So Te is the Third Plague. Dead that the monk resurrected start turning back into cadavers while Le So Te is convincing his fanatics to fight each other to death in order to find eternal life.

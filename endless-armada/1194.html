October 7th, 2004
1194 - Hundertsonnendämmerung
The Fall of the World of a Hundred Suns
Ernst Vlcek

Julian Tifflor and the GAVOK fleet organize an offensive against the Posbi world but their fleet is pushed back by the Elements of the Technology assisted by the Elements of the Mask, who have infiltrated the various Terran spaceships. During the fights, the Oxtornian Stalion Dove is exposed to the Hexadim Activator and thrown to an unknown location. The World of a Hundred Suns remains in control of Kazenkatt.

1115 - Bote des Unsterblichen
Message from the Eternity
Clark Darlton

On Eden 2, It gathers all the consciousnesses of the mutants and asks them to pick one of them to go warn the Earth about the danger coming from Vishna. They all pick Ernst Ellert, who is then informed of the situation and cast into the Milky Way. A radiation of howalgonium disturbs the trip and he ends up temporarily on Lepso, in the mind of a Free Trader. He finally manages to reach the Earth and to transmit the message to Julian Tifflor and Reginald Bull.

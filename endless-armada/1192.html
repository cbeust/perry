October 7th, 2004
1192 - Angriff auf die Hundertsonnenwelt
Attack on the World of a Hundred Suns
Arndt Ellmer

Kazenkatt intensifies his attacks on Gatas and the World of a Hundred Suns, which is threatened by the Element of Cold. Several production centers of the Posbis fall into the deficit world and the GAVOK unites forces with them to defend the central world against the Gruuthes.

Kazenkatta triggers the Hexadim Transmitter, a weapon from one of the bases of the Decalogue that causes the Hate Device to be reactivated. The Posbis turn against their allies, who are forced to abandon the planet, which then falls in the hands of Kazenkatt.

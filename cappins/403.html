403 - The Container Strasse
The Way of the Containers
H.G. Ewers

In order to maintain the stability of the Ghost System, the new name given to the Solar System displaced in the future, a connection by transmitters is established with Olymp, the second start of the Boscyk star. Big containers are exchanged daily, supplying the Terrans with food and various merchandises.

Olymp is directed by the emperor Anson Argyris, a Terran robot of the brand Vario-500 who is capable of human thoughts and possesses an individual consciousness. Challenged by the emperor Dabrifa, Anson Argyris manages to lie convincingly about the existence of the containers.

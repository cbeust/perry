402 - UFOs in der Galaxis
UFO's in the Galaxy
Clark Darlton

Unknown sphere that are four-kilometer big appear in the galaxy. Contact with Terran spaceships trigger spectacular explosions, leading the Terran scientist to think these ships are made of anti-matter, and that they might be coming from an anti-matter universe.

An observation mission sent on the planet Salem reveals that the spheres do not seem hostile and seem to be looking for something.

Nov 19, 2003
1017 - Auf den Spuren der Bruderschaft
On the Tracks of the Brotherhood
Kurt Mahr

On the spaceship, Mallagan attracts the attention of a Krane called Killsoffer, who tells them he is part of an organization called the Brotherhood. The Brotherhood only accepts bearers of several Spoodies as members and they fight the Duke of Krandhor. They arrive on a planet called Keryan, where a base of the Brotherhood is located. Chased by servants of the Duke of Krandhor, the Betschidians manage to hide in the city of Unadern.

March 23, 2003
1091 - Sperrgebiet Hyperraum
Forbidden Hyperspace
H.G. Ewers

When they rematerialize, Kitsaiman and his group discover a network of thousands of transmitters spanning over the whole Milky Way. After visiting several planets, they return to the Earth and report to Rhodan.

Through the resistance movement, Rhodan has a brief interview with the two Darghetians, whose health is deteriorating. In the meantime, scientists are busy studying the glove that he found on Khrat.

The Porleyters are requesting a fleet of two hundred thousand spaceships in order to launch a preemptive strike against a galaxy ruled by Seth Apophis. They blackmail the Terran organization by activating a "hyperspace destroyer", a device that makes it impossible for spaceships to use hyperspace in an area of twenty five thousand light years. Hundreds of spaceships suddenly become stranded.

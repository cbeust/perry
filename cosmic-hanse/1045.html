1045 - In den Höhlen von Lokvorth
In the Caves of Lokvorth
Peter Griese

On Lokvorth, Srimavo looks for Quiupu and finally locates him in remote caves in a forest. Quiupu has successfully reproduced a small fraction of the Viral Empire in the shape of a sphere, and he was also able to limit the Vishna component. Srimavo paralyzes Quiupu and tries to steal the sphere when Adelaies intervenes. A fight between Quiupu and Srimavo ensues and the young girl loses. Her body becomes transparent and she disapperas. During the fight, Ellmer came in contact with the sphere and received a strange vision.

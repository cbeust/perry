Jan 24, 2003
1063 - Ein Hauch von Leben
A Breath of Life
Detlev G. Winter

The mutants are feeling increasingly sick and Rhodan begins to suspect the cell activators of malfunction. The Dan Picot receives an impulse from a system nearby and heads there.

The inspection of the planet reveals a devastated civilization which is contrasted by the presence of a huge tree, from which the impulses came. The tree seems to be alive but no communication can be achieved. Rhodan discovers the two Darghetians in their crab-like bodies and takes them on board. As soon as the spaceship take off, the two crabs die.

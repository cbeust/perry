Nov 23, 2003
1012 - Der programmierte Mann
The Programmed Man
H.G. Francis

Bruke Tosen, a Springer, starts behaving strangely and a man called Gruude Vern comes to him to let him know he has been identifed as an agent of Seth Apophis. Tosen escapes but Felmer Lloyd finds him on a spaceship, where he left a bomb in an attempt to destroy the cruiser.

Lloyd manages to talk to Tosen who accepts to cooperate. The mutant takes Tosen back to the Earth for examination.

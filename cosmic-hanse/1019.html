Nov 19, 2003
1019 - In den Händen der Bruderschaft
In the Hands of the Brotherhood
Hans Kneifel

The three Betschidians are brought before the commander of the Brotherhood, Sargamec, a Tart whose mental state is severely perturbed by his second Spoodie. He submits Mallagan to tests which the Betschidian passes. A spy in the ranks of the Brotherhood betrays the location of the headquarters and soon, the Guard serving the Oracle invests it.

The three Betschidians escape and are rescued by Breborn, who wants to bring them to Krane to serve the Oracle. Breborn dies of his wounds soon thereafter.

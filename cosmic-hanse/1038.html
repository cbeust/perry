1038 - Der Verräter von Kran
The Traitor from Kran
Hans Kneifel

On Kran, the Dukes Gu, Carnuum and Capelrow are getting anxious at the delay of the Spoodie spaceship. The Oracle announces that one of the three Dukes is a traitor and he make them undergo various tests that lead to a final conflict during which the duke Capelrow is killed.

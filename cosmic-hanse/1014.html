1014 - Alles für die SOL
All for the Sol
H.G. Ewers

On their way to the Iktor, Mallagan and Faddon start showing signs of the Spoodie epidemic while Scoutie seems unharmed. In an underwater cave, they discover inanimate human bodies in suspended animation. They find out that the humanoids are able to project mental thoughts across the planet.

Five days later, they find the wreck of the Iktor.

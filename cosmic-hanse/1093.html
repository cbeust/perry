1093 - Testwelt Cheyraz
Test World Cheyraz
Detlev G. Winter

A study center called Stac has been charged to examine the glove brought back from Khrat. A young hyperphysicist called Josuar Gandaro manages to activate it, but a field test against a real Kardec Shield turns into a disaster: the experiment fails and the glove disintegrates.

Dec 9, 2003
1025 - Planet der Spiele
Planet of the Games
Marianne Sydow

Mallagan, Faddon and Scoutie are brought to Couhrs, where qualifying rounds for a popular game called the Lugosiade are being held. Thanks to his two Spoodies which give him extra reflexes, Mallagan makes a strong impression on a Krane trainer called Cylam. The Betschidians are then kidnapped by the Brotherhood, along with a game master. The kidnappers brainwash Mallagan in an attempt to make him serve the interests of the Brotherhood. The other two receive an extra Spoodie each.

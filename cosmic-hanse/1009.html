1009 - Agenten auf Mardi-Gras
Agents on Mardi Gras
Ernst Vlcek

Rhodan is quickly informed of the situation and Aerts sees an opportunity to escape and take control of the station. He teams up with Dino, the scientist that Aerts has identified as the author of the sabotage. He uncovers more agents from Seth Apophis but they eventually turn against him and wound him mortally. On the verge of dying, Aerts realizes he was an agent of Seth Apophis and tells Rhodan "they will try... to get more inside his depot..." before dying.

The destruction of the base can no longer be stopped. Rhodan collects a few samples of the virus and returns on the Earth to have them analyzed.

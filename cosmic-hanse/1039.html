Dec 10, 2003
1039 - Die Stimme der Bruderschaft
The Voice of the Brotherhood
Kurt Mahr

Scoutie and Faddon ask Seneca to see Mallagan. They are taken to a room where their friend is connected to hundreds of Spoodies through tubes. Seneca tells them he has created a supreme being, but the computer is unaware that the two Betschidians are now carrying several Spoodies themselves.

On Kran, the conflict between the two Dukes left escalates and the Brotherhood, which is trying to overthrow the Oracle, encourages this unrest. During an attack by the Brotherhood, the Duke Gu is seriously injured.

Finally, the Spoodie spaceship arrives on Kran.

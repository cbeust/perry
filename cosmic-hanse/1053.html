Jan 12, 2003
1053 - Metamorphose der Gläsernen
The Metamorphosis of the Glass Men
Peter Griese

On the Sol, the Buhrlos become increasingly agitated and insist on being taken to this mysterious location they call the Aura. The rest of the crew of the Sol is subject to a mysterious increase in creativity, except for the two hundred Betschidians.

Atlan finds out that the Buhrlos are the reason for the agitation and they are expelled from the Sol. They start heading toward the mysterious Aura and a shuttle sent from the Sol to examine them notices that their skin is slowly turning blue.

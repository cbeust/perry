1032 - Der Experimentalplanet
The Experimental Planet
Peter Griese

Convinced of the important of Quiupu's experimentations, Rhodan obtains fundings and offers the strange alien to work on the planet Lokvorth, the second planet of the sun Scarfaaru. Quiupu tells Rhodan that his goal is not just to revive the Viral Empire and that now, he needs to focus on an experience involving something called the Vishna Element.

Shortly after the Terran mission arrived on Lokvorth, Quiupu disappears.

March 5, 2003
1077 - Aura des Schreckens
Aura of Terror
Detlev G. Winter

Rhodan discovers that all the Kardec shields have disappeared, and the Porleyters are behaving increasingly hostile toward the Terrans. All of them are now wearing a Kardec shield, which gives them extra powers, such as teleportation and telekinetics.

The Terrans raise their shields but they are no match for the Porleyters' weapons. The Rakal Woolver is soon conquered and Rhodan and his team flee on a different spaceship. The Rakal Woolver takes off and heads for the Solar System.

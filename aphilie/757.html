757 - Welt ohne Menschen
World without People
William Voltz

The Earth, the Moon and Medallion emerge in an unknown region of space. This area is controlled by an incarnation called Clermac, which takes notice of the materialization of a solar system and starts monitoring it.

The arrival of the Earth has been observed by Douc Langur. Langur is a scientist on an exploration mission. He reports to an entity called the Empress of Therm and is guided by a portable computer called Logikor. Langur was initially part of a bigger space station called Module which disappeared. Langur is now stranded and when the solar system materializes in front of him, he decides to explore the Earth.

Alaska begins to realize that he seems to be the only person on Earth. He decides to head for Terrania.

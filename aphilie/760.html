Nov 5, 2001
760 - Kampf der Diplomaten
Fight of the Diplomats
Ernst Vlcek

Perry wants to get the Gavok on his side and starts a diplomatic mission in the main areas of influence of the Blues and the Antis. Atlan is pursuing the same agenda and tries to spread lies on Perry's intent. The old mutants, initially on Atlan's side, are beginning to have doubts about the Arkonide, but they keep supporting him for now.

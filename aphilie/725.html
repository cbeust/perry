725 - Allianz der Galaktiker
Alliance of the Galactics
Hans Kneifel

The conference begins and the negociations are tough. The Antis try to betray the rebels but their spaceship is destroyed by the mysterious Vhrato. All participants eventually agree to fight the Larens and the new rebel council, baptized Gavok, is created.

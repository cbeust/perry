May 23, 2002
766 - Der Herr der Welt
The Man of the World
Kurt Mahr

In February 3582, the deactivation of Nathan causes the loss of weather control on the Earth. Radio messages emanating from Terrania indicate that not all Terrans have disappeared from the surface of the Earth, and the former capital becomes the point of convergence of several survivors, among which Walik Kauk and Baldwin Tingmer.

Meanwhile, the mysterious black ship continues circling the Earth but is still avoiding contacts with the Terrans. The passenger keeps observing the Earth and relaying his findings to the Incarnation Clermac.

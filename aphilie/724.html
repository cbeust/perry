Jan 5, 2001
724 - Geheimkonferenz der Rebellen
Secret Conference of the Rebels
Kurt Mahr

The conference of the rebels is finally close at hand. All the guests are present on the planet, including the Halutians, who arrived at the last minute. However, the planet is just a bluff set up by Atlan, which fools both the Larens and the Heavies. As the planet is destroyed, the real guests of the conference are safely taken to the real location of the conference which can now begin.

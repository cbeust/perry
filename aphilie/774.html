May 23, 2002
774 - Die Stadt des Glücks
The City of Luck
Hans Kneifel

Worried that the presence of the humans are disrupting the balance of their civilization, the Tbahrgs demand that the Terrans leave. Rhodan still wants to investigate the hyperradio communication and refuses. The Tbahrgs capture several humans and make them hostage and they block the Sol on the surface of the planet. In retaliation, Atlan attacks some installations on Xumanth with the SZ-1.

The Tbahrgs become more open to negociations and explain to Rhodan that they are following orders from a higher race.

Jan 5, 2001
722 - Eine Botschaft für Ovaron
A Message for Ovaron
H.G. Ewers

The Dakkarkom is finally finished and Atlan sends a message for help in the distant Gruelfin galaxy. Things have changed a lot there since Rhodan visited it and the Ganjo civilization has degenerated. Ovaron is artificially maintained alive by a hostile government and while he remembers Rhodan and what he owes to the Terrans, the Ganjo government decides not to help the Terrans. Ovaron tries to escape but he dies in the attempt.

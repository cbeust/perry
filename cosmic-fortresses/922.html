922 - Invasion der Feuerkugeln
Invasion of the Fire Globes
Marianne Sydow

In February 3587, bright globes appear on the Earth and capture children from everywhere to bring them to their mother ship. The aliens are wearing blue suits and claim that they come from the Earth's future and that they need to save children from a forthcoming disaster. Dalanja, one of the kidnapped girls from India, does not believe them.

Thinking that the aliens are sent by Buddha, Dalanja's father ressurects the cult of Kali and kidnaps journalists who were investigating the UFO phenomenon.

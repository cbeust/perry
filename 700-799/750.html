June 29, 2001
750 - Ein Freund der Posbis
A friend of the Posbis
H.G. Francis

The Sol finally reaches the Milky Way. As Rhodan approaches the Earth, he witnesses a Posbi ship being attacked by two SVE-spaceships. He destroys the two Laren ships. Rhodan meets the commandant of the Posbi spaceship, a human called Galto Quohlfahrt, who has become part-posbi in an attempt to study the robotic race.

Quohlfahrt informs Rhodan of the situation in the Milky Way. Rhodan suspects that the New Einsteinian Empire is located in the Provcon-Faust dark cloud but he is shocked to learn that Atlan has decided to withdraw instead of fighting the Council.

732 - Aufstand der Immunen
Rebellion of the Immune
Hans Kneifel

As the Earth draws nearer to the Pharynx, expeditions are sent out to find a planet where the Terrans could escape. One of them discovers a civilization of dwarves living in unision with the nature. They help two of the immune crewmembers to heal from the Aphily. However, they both fail to overthrow the rest of the crew. The planet is destroyed along with its natives.

May 22, 2001
733 - Der Weg des Diktators
The Way of the Dictator
Hans Kneifel

Since Bully got cured from the Aphily, three candidates are fighting to take over his leadership on the Earth. Casalle receives the support of the OGN and is about to win the elections. However, he tries to deceive Roi Danton at the last minute. Danton manages to counter him and the traitor is imprisoned in Porta Pato.

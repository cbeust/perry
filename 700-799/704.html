704 - Die Rebellen von Imperium-Alpha
The Rebels from Imperium-Alpha
Ernst Vlcek

Decided to smash the resistance, Bull deploys a huge army, forcing the members of the OGN to flee to Borneo through a transmitter. Meanwhile, a positronic robot acts against its own kind in an effort to obey the first law of robotics, which says that no robots should ever harm humans.

More robots are freed and fly to Borneo, where they help the resistants. A traitor infiltrated in their ranks is uncovered and the resistants eventually find shelter in Porta Panos, the main headquarters of the non-Aphilians.
